SEP Tracker

An independent, cited tracker for the City of Greensboro's Strategic Energy Plan: Pathways to 100% Renewable Energy, and its 2025 progress.

Build status

This dashboard is under construction. The Water Resources department page is built as the reference pattern — trend vs. target, 2025 reporting status (including "not reported" and "deprioritized" states), and funding linkage, all with hover/tap citations.

Recommendations & Actions, Funding, and Timeline pages, plus this homepage's full rollup dashboard, are not yet built. The underlying data for all of them already exists and is fully cited — see data/index.json and data/SCHEMA.md.
